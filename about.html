About

Engineer is an annual technical fest symposium in India organized by NITK surathkal. The event was started adter the amalgamation of many technical festivals held at NITK Surathkal by student clubs. Held for the first time in 2005

Engineer provides the perfect platform for students across the globe to showcase their technical talents and their passion in the field of science and 6technology through events of international standards. It attracts enermous participation from students, subject experts, corporates and eminent personalities from all over the world. Each year, the festival surpasses its predecessor in terms of quality of events, industrial and entterpreneurial involment and participation.
